Service

Mayberry Electric has a culture of superior customer service. We are here to meet your needs 24/7, call our hotline day or night anytime at 404-645-7373. Our highly trained and experienced electricians are able to provide you the best service and we guarantee you will be satisfied with our efforts and feel great about the value we bring to your building, whether it be Commercial, Medical or Industrial.


Whether it's indoors or outdoors our expert electricians can handle any and all maintenance and upgrades to your lighting fixtures.

Troubleshooting can be risky and dangerous without the proper tools. Our electricians are always prepared with the proper gear to handle any troubleshooting issue.

Need a new outlet or socket installed in your commercial property? We come prepared with all kinds of different models and colors. We're sure to have what you need, just ask.

Wiring can be a messy business. Let our electricians expertly handle the headache for you.

Electrical components don't last forever. We recommend that you get an IR (Infrared) Scan done atleast once a year to ensure the safety of your system.

Pole lighting retrofiting and repairs is one of our specialties, ask about our services and our bucket trucks.

Getting you the proper information on your electricity usage is important to us. Let us handle setting up your meter systems.

We take pride in turning your parking garage into a bright and safe feeling parking experience for your patrons.

Arc Flash is an extraordinarily dangerous system malfunction. Our expert electricians are equiped with the proper gear to study and prevent this from happening to your system.

Looking to add EV Charging stations to your property, look no further. We have the tools and knowledge to install them in no time at all.

We take pride in verifying that your system is safe, efficiant and that it shows no sign of overloading.

Having a tough time deciding what kind of lighting fixtures to use in your space? Reach out and let us help make your decisions easier.
